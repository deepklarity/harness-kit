Build a workout logging app in React Native.

Main screen shows a weekly calendar strip at the top (horizontally scrollable, today highlighted). Below it, a list of exercises logged for the selected day. Each entry has exercise name, sets x reps, and optional weight.

Tap "+" to add an exercise. The add screen should let you pick from a short list of common exercises or type a custom name, then enter sets/reps/weight. Nothing complicated — just a clean form.

Long press an exercise to delete it. Persist with AsyncStorage. Show a small streak counter somewhere ("5 day streak") based on consecutive days with at least one logged exercise.

Dark mode by default. Should feel like a personal tool, not a gym brand app.
Rethink the app screens fresh and do not get limited by existing libraries installed.
