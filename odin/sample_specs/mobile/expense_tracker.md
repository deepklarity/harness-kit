Build a simple expense tracker app in React Native.

Add expenses with an amount, short description, and category (food, transport, entertainment, bills, other). Show a scrollable list of recent expenses grouped by date. At the top, show total spent this month and a simple breakdown by category — just colored bars, nothing fancy.

Swipe left on an expense to delete it. Tap to edit. Persist everything with AsyncStorage.

Keep the UI clean — white background, one accent color, clear typography. Should feel lightweight, not like an enterprise finance app.
